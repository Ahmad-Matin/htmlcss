Shanmugam says no influence by Liew Mun Leong in Parti Liyani case.

handled like other theft cases

SINGAPORE:

There was "pressure or influence exerted" by ex-Changi Airport Group chairman Liew Mun Leong or anyone acting on his behalf in the Parti Liyani case, Law and Home Affairs Minister K Shanmugam told Parliament on Wednesday (Nov 3).

“There was no influence by Liew Mun Leong,”

he said in a ministerial statement on the case. “It was treated as any other theft case and handled accordingly. We have checked with the investigating officer (IO), his senior officer and the Deputy Public Prosecutors (DPP)."
